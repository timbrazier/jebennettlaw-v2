JE Bennett Law are specialist vulnerable client lawyers based in Sevenoaks, Kent and we are very experienced in helping families where a loved one has dementia.



We understand that coming to terms with a loved one having dementia is a difficult time for all parties and we pride ourselves on our empathetic and professional approach. Depending on the personal circumstances of the person with dementia and the degree of that dementia there are a number of factors that need to be considered from a legal perspective and we'll help you address those that apply in your circumstances.
