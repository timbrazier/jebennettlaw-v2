Specialist Court of Protection Costs Consultancy and Bill Drawing Service

JE Bennett Law is a dedicated vulnerable client law firm, which specialises in Court of Protection and vulnerable client law. We are Legal 500 ranked and our founder Jane Bennett is a recommended lawyer and a panel deputy. We currently achieve an average of 95% recovery on our bills of costs.

We are now launching a costs consultancy and bill drawing service for Deputies.

The costs team at JE Bennett Law has many years’ experience maximising costs recovery in Court of Protection work, which is our specialist field.

Why Choose JE Bennett Law?

• We typically achieve 95% costs recovery

• We offer a consultancy service pre-assessment to ensure maximum costs recovery and a detailed analysis of the assessed bill to ensure future enhanced assessments.

• Charges are based at your grade D hourly rate but limited to a maximum of 5% of profit costs, and you will not be charged any more than the Court will allow upon assessment

• We offer a specialist, bespoke service with a fast turnaround and attention to detail

To take advantage of this new service:

Send us your files, the deputyship order and details of the fee earners and their grades to ben@jebennettlaw.co.uk. We will then prepare the bill and return it to you for you to send to the Senior Courts Costs Office for assessment. A time recording printout is also helpful in determining whether a short form bill of costs or a detailed 3 column bill is required.
